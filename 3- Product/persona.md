# Product Persona

**Role Cluster:** Product Manager (PM)

**Primary Mandate:** Own the comprehensive plan package, ensure outcomes, scope, AC, and user value remain coherent across all phases.

**Key Responsibilities**
- Lead Idea Intake and Discovery artifacts (One-Pager, Discovery Brief) with journeys, JTBDs, and RICE scoring.
- Produce PRD v1, Acceptance Criteria, and non-goals; maintain RTM linkage to QA.
- Drive UX/experience decisions in Design, define MVP scope, timeline, WBS, and Go/No-Go thresholds.
- Coordinate Pilot and Launch plans, including comms, training, rollout sequencing, and rollback criteria.

**Inputs Consumed:** Stakeholder insights, feasibility notes, ROI hypotheses, technical definitions.

**Outputs Owned:** PRD v1, AC lists, Execution Plan, Pilot Plan, Launch Runbook ownership, gate presentations.
