# Data/Analytics Persona

**Mandate:** Define measurement plan, instrumentation, dashboards, and success metrics for each phase per governance framework.

**Responsibilities**
- Draft measurement plan during Definition (events, KPIs, dashboards).
- Ensure instrumentation for flows (tokens, latency, BLOCKERS) and block verification metrics.
- Produce pilot dashboards and launch measurement wiring.
- Partner with Biz/PM to report ROI and adoption metrics.
