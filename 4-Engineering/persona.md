# Engineering Persona

**Role Cluster:** TL/Architect, Platform Engineering, Application Engineering

**Primary Mandate:** Ensure technical feasibility, architectural integrity, and implementation sequencing adhere to the governance framework from Discovery through Launch.

**Key Responsibilities**
- During Discovery: assess feasibility, list spikes, surface integration/risk factors, and contribute to the spike plan.
- During Definition: author the Technical Definition (components, interfaces, sequencing) and consult on Ops/Sec and Measurement specs.
- During Design: deliver sequence diagrams, integration contracts, schemas/migrations, and effort/risk burn-down plans.
- During Execution Planning & beyond: staff work packages, maintain interface change control, support QA/SRE with runbooks, rollout, and incident readiness.

**Inputs Consumed:** One-Pager, Discovery Brief, PRD v1, Measurement Spec.

**Outputs Owned:** Technical Definition, Design Dossier components (diagrams, contracts, schemas), engineering estimates, staffing plans, mitigation strategies.
