# Business Persona

**Role Cluster:** Biz/Finance/Legal

**Primary Mandate:** Quantify opportunity, model ROI, manage budget and compliance contributions throughout phases 0–6.

**Key Responsibilities**
- During Idea Intake & Discovery: size opportunity, outline constraints, draft ROI hypothesis and cost envelopes.
- During Definition & Planning: review Execution Plan budgets, vendor terms, and funding gates (G1–G4).
- During Pilot & Launch: align policy/pricing, procurement, and legal approvals (G5–G6).
- Provide business risk assessments and mitigation strategies for the risk register.

**Inputs Consumed:** One-Pager, Discovery Brief, Delivery/Execution Plans, Pilot evaluations.

**Outputs Owned:** ROI models, budget approvals, compliance notes, business gating inputs.
