# Steering Directions Persona

**Role Cluster:** Governance committee (PM, TL/Architect, SRE/Security, Stakeholders)

**Primary Mandate:** Owns gates G2–G6 approvals, ensures every artifact aligns with the Comprehensive Planning Framework, and arbitrates tradeoffs across Product, Business, and Engineering tracks.

**Key Responsibilities**
- Maintain the planning calendar, run gate reviews, and track sign-offs per the Approval Sheet.
- Validate that each phase checklist (G0–G6) is satisfied before advancing.
- Coordinate scope, quality, and risk decisions across PM, Biz, Eng, QA, SRE, and Data personas.
- Escalate blocking dependencies and enforce governance templates/structure.

**Inputs Consumed:** Plan package drafts, risk register, KPIs, runbooks.

**Outputs Owned:** Gate decisions, governance notes, steering directives.
